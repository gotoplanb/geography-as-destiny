## The Observation (Dave Stanton)

Anthony's *The Horse, the Wheel, and Language* describes a methodological tension within his own field. Historical linguistics — sometimes called archaeolinguistics — derives predictions about ancient languages and population movements from systematic comparative analysis of daughter languages. The predictions are theoretical and cannot be directly observed. Some archaeologists scoff at the work as illegitimate precisely because it cannot be directly verified through artifact recovery.

The pattern resembles the relationship between theoretical and experimental physics. The theoretical physicist derives predictions from mathematical models that cannot be directly observed. The experimental physicist designs instruments and experiments to test whether the predictions hold. The early twentieth century — possibly the largest explosion of knowledge in collective human history — happened because the two disciplines collaborated and respected each other's distinct contributions.

The Wald bomber framing is, ironically, a twentieth-century concept being applied to Bronze Age evidence gaps. But the irony is informative: the mechanism is structurally invariant across the temporal distance between WWII statistics and Bronze Age archaeology. The same survivorship-bias logic operates wherever evidence is selectively preserved.

## The Two-Discipline Mapping

| Theoretical Physics | Historical Linguistics |
|---|---|
| Mathematical models of unobserved phenomena | Comparative reconstruction of unrecorded languages |
| Predictions derived from known mechanisms | Predictions derived from systematic sound-change rules |
| Cannot be directly verified | Cannot be directly verified |
| Tells experimentalists where to point instruments | Tells archaeologists where to look |

| Experimental Physics | Archaeology |
|---|---|
| Designs instruments to test predictions | Designs excavations to test predictions |
| Confirms, modifies, or refutes theoretical claims | Confirms, modifies, or refutes linguistic reconstructions |
| Uncovers phenomena theory had not predicted | Uncovers phenomena reconstruction had not predicted |
| Generates anomalies that drive theoretical refinement | Generates anomalies that drive linguistic refinement |

The collaboration between the two disciplines is what produces durable knowledge in either case. Theoretical physics without experimental physics is mathematics that may or may not describe reality. Experimental physics without theoretical physics is data without organizing principles. Historical linguistics without archaeology is reconstruction that cannot be validated. Archaeology without historical linguistics is artifact distribution without organizing predictions about what the distribution should reveal.

**Theory tells you where to point the instruments.** The archaeologists who dismiss historical linguistics as illegitimate are operating in the same posture as the experimental physicists who once dismissed theoretical physics as mere mathematics — missing that the theory is what tells you what to look for, what to ignore, and what counts as a meaningful anomaly.

## The Wald Bomber Across Temporal Distance

Dave's note that the Wald bomber concept is being applied to Bronze Age evidence gaps captures something worth preserving. The Wald survivorship-bias correction was developed in 1943 to analyze WWII bomber damage. The structural mechanism it identifies — selective preservation producing biased samples that misdirect inference — operates across all periods and all evidence types. The conceptual tool's origin is twentieth century. Its applicability is universal across periods where preservation is selective.

This is the framework's analytical apparatus operating at its most powerful: tools developed for one analytical context apply to other contexts whenever the underlying structural mechanism is preserved. The Wald correction works for WWII bombers, for Bronze Age archaeology, for medieval historiography, for contemporary administrative records — anywhere selective preservation operates, the correction applies.

That is the framework as a portable apparatus. The cards developed for the Plains and Silk Road cases generate predictions about Bronze Age cases. The evidence types vary by period. The framework operates across the periods because the structural mechanism is preserved.
